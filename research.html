WELCOME TO THE RESEARCH PAGE

This research page compares three popular watch e-commerce websites i.e WatchShop, CreationWatches and Authentic watches focusing on their design, user interface, experience, features, and technologies. It provides a detailed analysis of how these websites work, what makes them unique, and how easy they are to use for customers. The goal is to understand their strengths and weaknesses and see which one offers the best shopping experience.

Navigation Bar

Designed in a simple and user friendly manner. Enters with a transition on opening the page or on the reload. Navigation button are cleary visible. On click on each button, the respective pages opens with a hover effect on it. Simple and unovercrowded nav bar with the access of ADD TO CART on top right side.



Website named "WATCHSHOP" has a decent style in navigation bar. But it seems a bit of confusion to the user. No easy access to the different Page unlike Watchify. The hover effect on the Categories makes a difficulties to the user.



Creation Watches serves user an overcrowded Navigation bar. All the contents are mentioned over the navigation bar creating a mess structure. Even the categories with distribution on it. On design, it looks quite satisfying with the theme and the color combination but the user interaction is quite disturbing.



Like the Creation Watches, The Authentic Watch is also overcrowded with the text. The first interaction with an user is quite confusing. There are individual navigation buttom for similar categories of Sales & Discount, Watch Categories and Brands. Basically, it lacks the distribution of every categories.
